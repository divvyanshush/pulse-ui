FAQ
Common questions
Is Pulse free?
Yes. Pulse is free to use. Sign up with email or Google to unlock bookmarks, saved searches, and the daily brief. No credit card required.
How often is the feed updated?
Every 30 minutes. The backend crawls all 20+ sources on a rolling schedule, scores new items, and pushes updates to the feed.
What is the heat score?
Heat is a composite score based on upvotes, comment count, engagement velocity, and cross-source mentions. Items decay over time so the feed stays fresh.
How are sources selected?
Sources are manually curated for signal quality. We prioritise sources with high technical depth and engaged communities. Quality over quantity.
Can I suggest a source?
Yes. If a source consistently produces high-quality AI content that Pulse is not covering, reach out and we will evaluate it.
Is there a mobile app?
The web app is fully responsive and works well on mobile. A native app is on the roadmap but not yet available.
