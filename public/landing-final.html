Things worth knowing before you try it.

Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial period, no freemium wall. Free to use.
In real time, automatically, across all 25+ sources. You don't need to refresh — new items appear at the top as they're ranked.
Every morning. It covers the highest-signal items from the previous 24 hours across every category — Models, Research, Tools, Repos, Funding, Policy. Open it first thing and you're already caught up.
It's Cobun's ranking algorithm. Each item gets a signal score based on four factors: source credibility, engagement velocity, developer relevance, and time decay. The result is a feed ranked by what matters to builders — not what gets the most clicks.
Manually curated for signal quality. Every source has to earn its place by consistently producing content that matters to developers and researchers. We prioritize technical depth and community engagement over traffic or brand name. New sources are reviewed regularly — suggest one on GitHub or Twitter.
You shouldn't take our word for it — open the feed and check the top 10 items against what you'd have found manually. If the signal score is working, you'll find things you would have missed and won't find things that wasted your time. That's the test.
The web app is fully responsive and works well on mobile. A native app is on the roadmap. For now, adding it to your home screen from mobile Safari or Chrome gets you most of the way there.
