PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Cobun AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform at cobunai.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Cobun AI:

  • Account information — email address and password (hashed) when you sign up
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and interactions via Google Analytics
  • Preferences — your saved searches, bookmarks, and display preferences
  • Authentication data — if you sign in with Google, we receive your email and profile name

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Cobun AI service
  • To sync your bookmarks and preferences across devices
  • To send email digests if you request them
  • To understand how users interact with the platform and improve it
  • To communicate important updates about the service

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely via Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database platform. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with industry-standard encryption.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Resend — email delivery for digests
  • Groq — AI-powered article summaries
  • Vercel — frontend hosting
  • Railway — backend hosting

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and analytics. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Cobun AI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@pulse.dev
